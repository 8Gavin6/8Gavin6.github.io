Mike's story
Mike is a billiards lover living in the 20th century who wanna become a professional billiards player. He trained every day and trained almost 8 hours every day. He trained five point training, snake colour and many other Basic spherical. After 8 years training, he become a professional billiards player. He said: at the beginning of the learning,it is so hard to found the hitting points and the stability of the shot. I spend almost 4 years to improve these skills into professional.
