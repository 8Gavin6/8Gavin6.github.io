Snooker World Top 10
Snooker World Top 10 Ronnie Antonio O'Sullivan is a professional snooker player from England, the reigning world champion and currently ranked number one in the world. He is also the holder of quite a few records in snooker, including being tied with Stephen Hendry for the most career-era world titles with seven. He is also widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of the sport.
